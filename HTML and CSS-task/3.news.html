March 24, 2026

Chennai Super Kings launched a special loyalty-membership programme for fans – ‘WSTL’ - during its historic Roar’26 event on March 22 at MA Chidambaram Stadium in Chennai. WSTL is designed to reward CSK fans, who have always stood by Chennai Super Kings. As part of it, the fans will receive a welcome kit, exclusive access to a loyalty store and early access to tickets. There will also be opportunities for select fans to attend meet and greet sessions with CSK players as part of the package. To become a WSTL member, download the official Chennai Super Kings App and register. The first ever WSTL membership was given to Super Fan Saravanan by Chennai Super Kings’ skipper Ruturaj Gaikwad at Roar 2026. Speaking about WSTL, Chennai Super Kings’ MD KS Viswanathan said: “We’ve always believed that our fans are the heartbeat of CSK, and their unwavering support over the years has been truly incredible. With the launch of WSTL, we wanted to create a meaningful way to give back to them through a dedicated fan loyalty programme. This initiative is designed to reward their passion, bringing them even closer to the team they love.”
